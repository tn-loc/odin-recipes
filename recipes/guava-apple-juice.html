Juice Recipes

Can't enjoy a meal with a fresh drink, do ya ?

Ingredients

  • 2 medium sized Guava
  • 1 large Apple
  • 1 fresh ginger

Steps

  1. Cut guava and apple into chunks peel ginger.
  2. Blend with 1 liter of cold water
  3. Sieve with a clean mesh
  4. Add a little bit of sugar if you want it to be sweeter
  5. Add ice for the cool
  6. Enjoy!
